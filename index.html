Define Hobbies

Once you have installed the app, you will be prompted to add hobbies that you would like to keep track of. By default, we assume that you can dedicate at least 4 hours per day to your hobbies.

Use Pomodoro

Enhance your hobby experience by incorporating the Pomodoro technique into your routine. Utilize the convenient built-in Pomodoro timer within Trackr, allowing you to allocate specific time intervals for dedicated hobby sessions.

Reflect

Take a moment to reflect on how effectively you have been allocating your time to pursue your hobbies. Establish a personal benchmark and strive to continually improve with each passing day.

Maximize Productivity.

Tracking and optimizing your use of time is essential for maximizing productivity and achieving your goals.
